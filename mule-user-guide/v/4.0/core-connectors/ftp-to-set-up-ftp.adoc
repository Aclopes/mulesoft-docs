Anypoint Studio, Design Center: FTP connector

[[short_description]]
An FTP configuration is required for all operations with the FTP connector.

. Open the `FTP Configuration` window.
+ You can open it from any FTP operation in your flow or from the *Connections Explorer* in Anypoint Studio.
. Provide a *Name* for the configuration. Example: `FTP configuration`.
. Provide connection settings:
  ** Provide the connection *Type*:
    *** `FTP Connection`
    *** `SFTP Connection`
  ** *Host* domain name or server IP address. Example: `ftp://somecompany.com`
  ** *Port* for the transport protocol. Defaults to `21`.
  ** *Password* if required by the host.
. Provide a *Working Directory*. Example: `my/working/dir`
. Provide a *Transfer Mode* (one of these):
  ** `Binary` (default)
  ** `Ascii`
. Indicate whether to use *Passive* mode. Defaults to `true`.

For other configuration options, go to link:#see_also[See Also].
