For Anypoint Studio, Design Center: link:email-about-the-email-connector[Email] connector

[[short_description]]
Eliminates (expunges) from a mailbox folder all email that is flagged for deletion.

[[expunge_folder]]
. After adding the *Email* connector to your flow, select the *Expunge Folder* operation.
